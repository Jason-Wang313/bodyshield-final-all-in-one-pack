\section{Scope}
This appendix records anticipated reviewer concerns. The current artifact is
non-hardware and should not be read as a final hardware-result submission.

\section{Key Replies}
BodyShield is distinct from domain randomization because it searches for
policy-specific breaking perturbations before repair. It is distinct from
generic counterexample-guided repair because the counterexamples are
body/control changes whose feasibility is checked with an oracle policy.

\section{Remaining Risks}
The largest risk is transfer from analytic and bounded simulator evidence to
real contact-rich hardware. The paper should remain scoped until hardware
results, verifier audits, reset reliability, and noise-floor measurements exist.
